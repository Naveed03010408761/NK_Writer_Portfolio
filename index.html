The Writer / Ù…ØµÙ†Ù

Naveed Khan Safi is an emerging Urdu script and fiction writer whose work explores the delicate balance between faith, love, morality and human psychology.

His storytelling blends spiritual depth with emotional intensity, often placing characters at the crossroads of desire, duty and destiny. Through philosophical undertones and socially grounded narratives, he aims to create stories that not only entertain but awaken reflection.

Writing primarily in Urdu, Naveed's unique voice draws from the rich traditions of Urdu literature while addressing contemporary social and spiritual conflicts. His upcoming book, Mot ka Raqs, is a philosophical mirror held up to society, while his television scripts, Mohrat and Humraaz, explore the complexities of love, faith, and the supernatural.

Currently working on completing his book and developing his scripts for the screen, he is actively seeking collaborations with production houses that value meaningful, layered storytelling.
